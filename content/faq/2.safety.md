## How does Michi keep things safe?
In order to keep its users safe, Michi only accepts sign-ups from Google and Apple accounts. We also encourage having a profile picture to easily identify each other upon first meets.

## How do I report someone?
Open their profile, tap the menu, choose Report. Reports go to a real person and we respond within 24 hours.

## What happens if I block someone?
They can't see your profile, message you, nor join any events you're in. If you block someone, they aren't told.

## Can I see who else is going before I show up?
Yes. Every event shows the host, the headcount, and the other RSVPs before you join.

## What if someone no-shows?
Hosts can flag no-shows. Repeated no-shows lower account standing and limit access to popular events.

## Is my exact location ever shared?
No. We dont share users nor event locations to others. Events presented to users only shows the erea where the event is planed to take place. The adress is shown once someone joins the events.
