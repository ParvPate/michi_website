## How do I create an event?
Tap the plus icon on the top right of the Discover page to begin creating an Event. From there, pick a place, set a time, write a one-line description and invite friends to join your events.

## How do I join an event?
You can join event that appears in your Discovery page or events that have been shared to you by tapping the join button. If the events was sent to you, you will be placed in the event immediately. If not, a request will be sent to the host and you'll be placed in the event once the host accepts your request. Once you're in the event, you can view the details and chat with other attendees.

## Can I cancel an RSVP?
Up to 2 hours before the event starts. After that, you're marked as no-show if you don't make it.

## What kinds of events work best?
We suggest being specific activity and its location. of the evnets a, doable and public events work best. "Coffee at Maru, Saturday 10am" beats "Anyone want to grab coffee sometime?" The clearer the event, the more likely people are to show up.

## What happens to the group chat after?
It quiets down 24 hours after the event ends. However, you will still be able to open it.

## Can I host a recurring event?
Yes. When you create an event, choose "weekly" or "every other week."
