## How do I delete my account?
Go to your profile, then into Settings, then Account, and press the "Delete account" button.

## How do I change my phone number?
Go to your profile, then into Settings, then Account, and press the "Delete account" button. We'll send you a text message. You'll verify the new number by text.

## Why is the friend list capped?
Because Michi is about the people you actually see, not the people you've ever met. The cap is 50.

## Can I export my data?
Yes. Settings → Privacy → Request data export. We send a download link by email within 7 days.

## Will Michi sell or share my data?
No. Michi has no ads and no data partners. Your contact list, location, and activity stay on your account — see the privacy policy for the full breakdown.

## Will Michi notify me when my friends post?
There are no posts. Michi notifies you about plans, not activity feeds.
