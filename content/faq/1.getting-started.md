## What is Michi?
Michi is a real-life social app. It shows you what's happening near you tonight, this weekend, this month — and helps you actually go.

## How do I sign up?
Download Michi from the App Store or Play Store. Verify your phone number. Pick a few things you'd actually do. You're in.

## What does Michi cost?
Michi is free. No ads, no feed, no data sales.

## Where does Michi work?
We're rolling out city by city. Open the app to see if your area is live yet — or join the waitlist for it. New cities open every month.

## Do I need to be 18?
Yes. Michi is 18+.

## What if my friends aren't on Michi?
That's the point. Michi is built for meeting people you don't know yet — through the things you both decided to show up for. You don't need a network to start.
