## What is Michi?
Michi is a real-life social app that shows you what's happening near you. It encourages you to meet people by showing you real plans, posted by real people, within walking distance of where you're standing right now. No feed, no scrolling, no algorithm deciding what you see. 

## How do I sign up?
You can download Michi from the App Store or Play Store. You'll need either a Google or Apple account, pick a few interests and select moments throughout the week you're likely to be active. 

## What does Michi cost?
Michi is free social app. It doesn't cost a dime nor does it have ads.

## Where does Michi work?
We're rolling out city by city. Open the app to see if your area is live yet, or join the waitlist for it. New cities open every month.

## Do I need to be 18?
Yes. Michi requires you to be 18+.

## What if my friends aren't on Michi?
Good, that's the  point. Michi isn't for managing the people you already know. It's for meeting the ones who showed up to the same thing you did. You don't need a network to start.
